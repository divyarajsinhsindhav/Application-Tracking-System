About Us

Welcome to our Application Tracking System (ATS), a pioneering solution designed to establish and promote good governance for the Rajkot Gram and Jilla Panchayat. We are committed to transforming the way local governments manage and monitor applications, ensuring transparency, efficiency, and citizen-centric services.

Our Mission

At ATS, our mission is to empower local governments with the tools and technology they need to enhance the lives of their citizens. We believe in the power of modern technology to streamline administrative processes, eliminate bureaucracy, and facilitate open communication between government institutions and the people they serve.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a more transparent and accessible government system that promotes good governance. We envision a future where citizens can easily track the progress of their applications, access information, and provide feedback, making the administrative process seamless and efficient.

Why ATS?

Our team is comprised of passionate professionals who believe that technology can be a catalyst for positive change. We have a deep understanding of the challenges local governments face and the needs of citizens. ATS is the result of our dedication to creating a system that bridges the gap between citizens and government entities, enabling both parties to work together towards a more efficient and transparent society.
